## Product Description

The SonoTech Ultrasonic Spray Coating System combines a precision ultrasonic atomizer with a programmable XY motion stage to deliver repeatable, uniform thin-film coatings. The system is designed for research laboratories and pilot production lines requiring precise control over coating thickness, uniformity, and material consumption.

## Key Features

- **Programmable XY stage** with 0.01 mm positioning accuracy
- **Interchangeable nozzle frequencies** (25, 40, 60, 120 kHz) for different liquid viscosities
- **Integrated syringe pump** with flow rate control from 0.1 mL/min
- **Substrate heating stage** (optional, up to 200°C) for solvent evaporation during coating
- **Recipe storage** — save and recall coating programs for reproducibility
- **Closed-loop feedback** on generator output power

## Applications

This system is widely used for:

- PEM fuel cell MEA catalyst layer deposition
- Electrolyzer electrode coating (PEM, AEM, alkaline)
- Lithium-ion battery electrode coating
- Perovskite and CIGS solar cell fabrication
- Medical device surface functionalization
- OLED and thin-film display manufacturing
