Ultrasonic Equipment for Battery Manufacturing

Lithium-ion battery performance is critically dependent on the quality of electrode slurry preparation and coating uniformity. Ultrasonic homogenizers and spray coating systems provide superior dispersion of active materials, conductive additives, and binders, resulting in more uniform electrode microstructure and improved battery performance.

Battery Electrode Slurry Preparation

Ultrasonic homogenization replaces or supplements ball milling for electrode slurry preparation, offering faster processing, better particle size control, and reduced contamination. Key advantages include:

  • 60% reduction in processing time vs. ball milling
  • Achieves D50 < 1 μm particle size for improved electrode density
  • Better dispersion of carbon nanotubes (CNT) and graphene conductive additives
  • No metallic contamination from grinding media
  • Scalable from lab (100 mL) to production (200 L) with flow-through reactors

Solid-State Battery Thin Film Coating

For solid-state battery development, ultrasonic spray coating enables precise deposition of solid electrolyte thin films (LLZO, LIPON, sulfide-based) and electrode coatings with sub-micron thickness control.
