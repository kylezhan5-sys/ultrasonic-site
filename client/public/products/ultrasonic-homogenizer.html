Ultrasonic Homogenizer

SonoTech ultrasonic homogenizers deliver powerful cavitation for emulsification, dispersion, cell disruption, and nanoparticle synthesis. 50W-3000W, 20-40 kHz.

Product Overview

The SonoTech Ultrasonic Homogenizer uses high-intensity ultrasonic cavitation to process liquids at the molecular level. When the ultrasonic probe vibrates at 20-40 kHz, it creates millions of microscopic cavitation bubbles that collapse violently, generating intense local pressure and temperature. This cavitation energy is highly effective for emulsification, particle dispersion, cell disruption, and accelerating chemical reactions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Probe sonication delivers focused, high-intensity ultrasonic energy directly into the sample via a vibrating probe, making it far more efficient for processing small to medium volumes. Bath sonication provides lower intensity distributed across a water bath, suitable for gentle cleaning or simultaneous processing of multiple sealed containers.

Power selection depends on sample volume, viscosity, and desired processing intensity. As a general guide: 50-100W for 1-100 mL, 300-500W for 100 mL-2 L, 1000W+ for 2-20 L. Our technical team can recommend the optimal configuration based on your specific material and target particle size.
