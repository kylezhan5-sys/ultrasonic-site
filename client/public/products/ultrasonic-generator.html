Ultrasonic Generator

SonoTech digital ultrasonic generators with auto-frequency tracking. 100W-5000W, 15-200 kHz. Compatible with all transducer types. CE certified.

Product Overview

The SonoTech Digital Ultrasonic Generator is the power supply and control unit that drives ultrasonic transducers. It converts standard AC power into a high-frequency electrical signal precisely matched to the transducer resonant frequency. The auto-frequency tracking (AFT) system continuously monitors and adjusts the output frequency to maintain optimal resonance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Auto-frequency tracking (AFT) continuously monitors the transducer operating frequency and automatically adjusts the generator output to maintain resonance. This is important because the resonant frequency changes slightly with temperature, load, and aging. Without AFT, the system would operate off-resonance, reducing efficiency and potentially damaging the transducer.

In most cases, one generator drives one transducer to maintain optimal frequency matching and power control. For ultrasonic cleaning applications, one generator can drive multiple transducers connected in parallel if they are matched in frequency and impedance.
