Ultrasonic Sewing Machine

SonoTech ultrasonic sewing machines bond and cut synthetic fabrics and non-woven materials without needles or thread. Ideal for medical, hygiene, and technical textile production.

Product Overview

The SonoTech Ultrasonic Sewing Machine bonds synthetic fabrics and non-woven materials using ultrasonic energy instead of conventional needles and thread. The system simultaneously bonds and trims fabric edges, creating sealed seams that are stronger than stitched seams and completely sealed against liquid penetration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Ultrasonic sewing works with thermoplastic synthetic fabrics including polypropylene (PP), polyester (PET), polyethylene (PE), nylon, and spunbond/meltblown non-wovens. Natural fibers (cotton, wool) and non-thermoplastic materials cannot be ultrasonically bonded.

For synthetic non-woven materials, ultrasonic bonding typically produces seam strength equal to or greater than the base material strength, and the sealed seam is impermeable to liquids - a significant advantage for medical and hygiene applications.
