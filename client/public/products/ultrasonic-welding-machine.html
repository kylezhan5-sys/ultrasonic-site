Welding

Ultrasonic Welding Machine

SonoTech ultrasonic welding machines provide fast, clean thermoplastic joining for automotive, medical, and electronics assembly. 20 kHz / 30 kHz / 40 kHz, 500W-3000W.

Product Overview

The SonoTech Ultrasonic Welding Machine joins thermoplastic parts using high-frequency mechanical vibration. The ultrasonic energy is focused at the joint interface, generating frictional heat that melts and fuses the plastic surfaces in milliseconds. The result is a strong, clean weld without adhesives, solvents, or fasteners.

Frequently Asked Questions

Ultrasonic welding works best with semi-crystalline and amorphous thermoplastics including ABS, PC, PP, PE, PA (nylon), POM, PVC, and PMMA. Dissimilar plastics can sometimes be welded if they have compatible melting temperatures. Thermosets and rubbers cannot be ultrasonically welded.

Lower frequency (20 kHz) systems produce higher amplitude vibration and are better suited for larger, harder, or more rigid parts. Higher frequency (40 kHz) systems produce lower amplitude with more precise energy control, ideal for delicate, small, or thin-walled components.
