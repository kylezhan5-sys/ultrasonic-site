Ultrasonic Transducer

SonoTech ultrasonic transducers convert electrical energy to mechanical vibration. Available in 15-200 kHz, 100W-5000W. Compatible with all major generator brands.

Product Overview

The SonoTech Ultrasonic Transducer is the core energy conversion component in any ultrasonic system. It converts electrical energy from the generator into mechanical vibration using high-performance piezoelectric ceramics (PZT). SonoTech transducers are precision-machined from titanium alloy and feature optimized acoustic design for maximum energy transfer efficiency and long service life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Signs of transducer failure include reduced output power, abnormal heating, visible cracks in the ceramic stack, or the generator showing high current draw with low amplitude output. Regular impedance testing can identify degradation before complete failure.

SonoTech transducers can be made compatible with most major generator brands including Branson, Herrmann, Rinco, and Telsonic by matching the impedance and frequency specifications. Please provide your generator model and we will confirm compatibility.
